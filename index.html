History of UFC

The Ultimate Fighting Championship (UFC) is the largest mixed martial arts organization in the world. It was created in 1993 with the goal of determining the most effective martial art in a real fight. Today, the UFC has become a global phenomenon, featuring some of the world's top fighters and highly anticipated events.
